# Verdocast — Office / B2B Marketing Playbook

**Stream:** Office / B2B prediction leagues
**Goal of this stream:** convert free group-stage leagues into **paid per-league knockout unlocks** (admin pays once, whole team keeps playing).
**Window:** written 13 Jun 2026 · group stage now · knockout paywall lands **28 Jun (R32)** · final **19 Jul**.

> The entire B2B game is a **calendar play**. The free group stage is acquisition and habit-building; the knockout cutover (28 Jun) is the single moment money changes hands. Everything below is organised around getting as many leagues as possible *activated* before that date so the unlock is a no-brainer.

---

## 1. North Star & funnel

**North-star metric:** number of leagues that **convert to a paid knockout unlock.**

The funnel, with the metric that gates each step:

| Stage | What happens | Watch this |
|---|---|---|
| **Acquire** | Admin creates a free league | Leagues created / week |
| **Activate** | Members join and predict ≥1 group match | % of leagues with ≥60% members predicting |
| **Habituate** | Members return across group-stage match-days | Avg predictions per member |
| **Convert** | Admin pays the knockout unlock before 28 Jun | **Unlock conversion rate** |
| **Retain/Expand** | League plays the knockouts; admin advocates | Knockout-round return rate, referrals |

The leverage point is **Activate → Habituate**. An admin will not pay to continue something their team ignored. A league where 25 of 30 people predicted every group match sells itself on 28 June. So most "marketing" spend before the cutover should go into *activation*, not top-of-funnel ads.

---

## 2. The buyer (ICP) and what they actually want

**Primary buyer:** HR / People / Internal-Comms / Office Manager / EA. Often the person who already runs the socials, the pub quiz, the Christmas do.

**They are not a sports-betting customer.** Do not sell odds, accumulators, or "beat the bookies." Sell **team ritual and belonging**.

**Jobs they're hiring Verdocast to do:**
1. Create connection across a hybrid/remote/distributed team with near-zero effort.
2. Get a visible culture win they personally get credit for.
3. Include *everyone* — the person who's never watched a match can still pick scores and win.
4. Do it without IT, procurement, or a data-protection fight.

**Top objections (pre-empt all of these — see §8):**
- "Can I justify the spend?"
- "Is this gambling / is it appropriate at work?"
- "Will people actually use it?"
- "How much of my time does it take to run?"
- "What about employee data / GDPR?"

## 3. Positioning & message

**One-liner:** *Turn the World Cup into your team's ritual — a free prediction league anyone can play, live in two minutes.*

**The narrative:** Big tournaments are one of the few moments a whole company naturally cares about the same thing at the same time. Verdocast captures that and turns it into a daily reason for people to talk to each other — fully inclusive (it's score *predictions*, not football knowledge), zero admin, and free to start.

**Message pillars:**
1. **Effortless to run** — one link, no spreadsheets, scores update automatically.
2. **Everyone can play** — non-football people win all the time; it's predictions, not trivia.
3. **Built for hybrid teams** — the leaderboard is the new watercooler.
4. **The knockouts are the payoff** — groups hook them; the unlock keeps the rivalry alive to the final.

**Voice (per brand):** confident, analytical, lightly editorial — *The Athletic, not DraftKings.* Hyphens, not em-dashes.

**Words to use:** ritual, rivalry, leaderboard, watercooler, inclusive, two minutes, free to start, your team.
**Words to avoid:** bet, odds, stake, gamble, wager, punter, accumulator.

## 4. The campaign calendar (this is the spine)

### Phase A — Land grab · now → ~24 Jun (group stage)
Goal: maximise **leagues created and activated** while the product is free. Every activated league is a pre-qualified knockout sale.
- Push "start a free league" everywhere.
- Hammer **activation** inside existing leagues (admin invites team → members predict).
- Seed share/referral loops so admins recruit other admins.

### Phase B — Conversion window · ~24 Jun → 28 Jun
Goal: convert activated leagues to the paid unlock. This is the money fortnight-in-miniature.
- Switch admin messaging from "invite your team" to **"unlock the knockouts."**
- Run **early-bird pricing** that expires at the R32 kickoff (28 Jun) — a real deadline, not a fake one.
- Lead every touch with the league's *own* engagement numbers.

### Phase C — Knockout retention & advocacy · 28 Jun → 19 Jul
Goal: keep unlocked leagues engaged, collect proof, capture demand you can reuse.
- Per-round digests ("R16 is set — here's who's still in your league").
- Ask happy admins for testimonials / a one-line quote + logo.
- Capture leagues that *didn't* unlock onto a "tell me about next time" list for future products (editorial sub, next tournament).

## 5. Channels, ranked for a 15-day, low-budget sprint

1. **Email lifecycle to existing free admins — highest ROI.** You already have a warm base and Resend is wired. This is the workhorse for conversion. (Full sequence in §6.)
2. **LinkedIn (organic, founder-led).** Where People/HR leaders live. Founder POV posts + genuine engagement in HR threads beat any ad. Templates in §7.
3. **HR / People communities.** People Geeks, Hacking HR, Lattice/Culture Amp communities, r/humanresources, local HR Slack/WhatsApp groups. Show up as a helpful peer with a free tool, not an ad.
4. **Referral / share loop (already built).** Per-creator branded leagues + `?ref=` tracking. Turn every admin into a recruiter of other admins ("invite a mate's company").
5. **SEO (already shipped).** The `world-cup-2026` group/team pages capture "world cup office predictor / sweepstake" intent. Add a dedicated "World Cup office prediction league" landing angle if time allows.
6. **Direct founder outreach.** A focused list of 50–100 warm companies (your network, alumni, ex-colleagues) — a personal note converts far above cold.
7. **Partnerships (opportunistic).** Office-perks platforms, HR tooling newsletters, coworking spaces. Slack/Teams integration is v2, but you can still pitch "drop the link in your team channel."

## 6. Email lifecycle (the conversion engine)

Triggered, not blasted. Every send leads with the league's own data where possible. Keep them short, editorial, one clear CTA.

**To the ADMIN:**

| # | Trigger | Angle | Subject line (example) |
|---|---|---|---|
| A1 | League created, 0 invites | Get your team in | "Your league's empty — fix that in 30 seconds" |
| A2 | Invited but <40% joined | Activation nudge | "{Name}, 4 of your 12 have joined" |
| A3 | Group match-day digest (recurring) | Habit + FOMO | "Match-day: who's top of {League} right now" |
| A4 | ~5 days before R32 | **Early-bird unlock** | "Keep {League} alive for the knockouts" |
| A5 | 48h before R32 | Deadline | "Groups end Saturday — unlock before the price goes up" |
| A6 | R32 kickoff, not unlocked | Last call | "The knockouts start today. Your league is paused." |
| A7 | Unlocked | Confirmation + hype | "{League} is in. Here's the knockout bracket." |
| A8 | Each knockout round | Retention | "R16 is set — 3 of your players still have a perfect run" |

**To the PLAYER (they don't pay, but they pressure the admin):**

| # | Trigger | Angle |
|---|---|---|
| P1 | Joined | "You're in. Make your first picks." |
| P2 | Missed a match-day | "You left points on the table yesterday." |
| P3 | Pre-R32 | "The knockouts are about to start — nudge your admin to unlock." ← *players asking the admin to pay is your best conversion lever.* |

> A 30-person team on Verdocast made 410 predictions in the first week. The most-used Slack channel in the company right now is the one arguing about the leaderboard.
> This is what "engagement" actually looks like — and it costs nothing to start.

**Post 3 — the conversion (Phase B):**
> Group stage ends Saturday. Every office that ran a free prediction league now faces the obvious question: do we keep it going for the knockouts?

**Comment-to-DM motion:** ask people to comment a keyword; DM the link + a personal note. Higher reach than links-in-post, and it warms the lead.

## 8. Objection-handling cheat-sheet

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "Is this gambling?" | No. It's free to play, score predictions only, no entry fees and no prize pots handled by us. Think office sweepstake without the cash or the admin. |
| "Can I justify the cost?" | One payment covers your whole team for the entire knockouts — cheaper than a single round of coffees, and it's a culture win people actually remember. |
| "Will people use it?" | Look at your own group-stage numbers (we'll show them to you). If they predicted the groups, they'll want the knockouts. |
| "How much of my time?" | Setup is two minutes and one link. Scores update automatically. There's nothing to run. |
| "Employee data / GDPR?" | We only need an email to send the magic link. No passwords, no sensitive data, no marketing to your colleagues. |
| "Too late to start?" | Start free for the knockouts directly — a fresh leaderboard from R32 is its own clean competition. |

## 9. The unlock offer (how to present price)

- **Early-bird, deadline = 28 Jun (R32 kickoff).** Real urgency tied to a real event.
- **Priced per league by size** — reuse the existing `max_members` tiers (small team / company / enterprise). One price, no per-round upsells (off-brand).
- **Value frame:** "the whole knockout game (R32 → final) + your leaderboard carried over, for your entire team."
- **Risk reversal:** lightweight money-back if the league doesn't play a single knockout match — removes the "what if they lose interest" fear.
- **Players never pay.** Say it explicitly, every time — it protects the free/viral engine and removes the admin's social risk of asking colleagues for money.

## 10. Targets & instrumentation

Use `/admin/stats` (already built) as the daily dashboard. Track:
- Leagues created / day (Phase A)
- Activation rate: % leagues with ≥60% members predicting
- Predictions per member (habit proxy)
- **Unlock conversion rate** (north star)
- Referral-attributed leagues (via `?ref=`)

Suggested weekly rhythm: one number per phase on a whiteboard, reviewed daily during the conversion window. If activation is low, *stop* doing top-of-funnel and fix activation — unconverted dead leagues won't pay.
